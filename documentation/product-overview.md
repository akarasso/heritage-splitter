# Product Overview

## What is Heritage Splitter?

Heritage Splitter is a decentralized platform built on **Avalanche** that enables artists and producers to collaboratively create, manage, and sell NFT collections with **transparent and automated revenue distribution**.

The platform solves a fundamental problem in the art world: when multiple creators collaborate on a collection, managing revenue splits, royalties, and rights transfers becomes complex and opaque. Heritage Splitter makes this trustless and automatic through smart contracts.

Every payment — whether from a primary sale or secondary royalty — is split on-chain according to pre-agreed allocations, with no intermediary and no manual distribution.

## The Problem

In the traditional art world:
- Revenue distribution between collaborators is manual, delayed, and error-prone
- Producers and galleries take opaque commissions with no verifiable accounting
- Rights transfer agreements require expensive legal intermediaries
- Artists have no visibility into secondary sales or royalty enforcement
- Physical galleries cannot easily bridge to digital/NFT markets

## The Solution

Heritage Splitter provides:

1. **On-chain revenue splitting** — Every payment is split automatically by smart contracts. Shares are defined in basis points (10,000 = 100%) and enforced immutably.

2. **Collaborative governance** — All participants must approve allocation terms before deployment. No surprises, no disputes.

3. **Producer showrooms** — Digital storefronts where producers curate artist collections, set their margin, and sell to collectors. One transaction covers base price + margin.

4. **Document certification** — Upload contracts, certificates, or any document and get an on-chain SHA-256 timestamp proof. Tamper-proof, legally verifiable.

5. **ERC-2981 royalties** — Secondary sale royalties enforced via the industry standard. Compatible with all major marketplaces.

## Key Features

### For Artists
- Create projects and invite collaborators
- Upload and mint NFT collections (ERC-721)
- Define allocation shares with all participants
- Certify documents on-chain (EIP-712 signatures)
- Share collections with producer showrooms
- Receive automatic payments on every sale

### For Producers
- Create showrooms (digital storefronts)
- Invite artists and curate their collections
- Set margins on top of base prices
- Deploy showroom as an on-chain contract
- Publish public sale pages (no account needed to buy)
- Track sales and margins via dashboard

### Platform
- Wallet-based authentication (MetaMask)
- 3-step KYC onboarding with role selection
- Real-time WebSocket notifications
- Threaded discussions on projects and collections
- Direct messaging between users
- Full audit logging
